## Killer Features to Implement

### 1. The Pitch Deck Generator
One-click generate investor pitch deck from strategy.
- Uses all analysis done
- Professional template
- Auto-fills metrics
- Download as PDF

**Virality:** Founders share on Twitter, tag investors

### 2. Growth Scorecard
Rate founder on 10 key metrics.
```
Product-Market Fit:      7/10
Growth Potential:        8/10
Competitive Position:    6/10
Pricing Optimization:    5/10
Team Readiness:          7/10
Market Size:             9/10
Time to Profitability:   6/10
```
Shareable. Engaging. Shows gaps.

### 3. Founder Score  
Rate probability of success (0-100).
```
Your Founder Score: 72/100
You're in the top 25% of founders analyzing markets.
```
Creates engagement loops.

### 4. Industry Benchmarks
Compare against similar companies.
```
Your SaaS vs Industry Average:
- TAM: You $2.5B vs Avg $1.8B (✓ above)
- CAC: You $500 vs Avg $850 (✓ excellent)
- LTV: You $12K vs Avg $15K (→ room to improve)
```
Shows value. Identifies gaps.

### 5. The Opportunity Report
Deep analysis of market gaps.
```
Your market has 3 major gaps:
1. No affordable solution for SMEs ($800M TAM)
2. Poor mobile experience in competitors
3. No vertical solution for agencies ($200M niche)
```
Shows WHERE to win.
