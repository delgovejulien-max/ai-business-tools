THE CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT SYSTEM
================================================================================

CORE PRINCIPLE
Every week, ship 1 validated improvement -> 1% better
Compound effect: 52 weeks x 1% = 57% improvement by year end

WEEKLY RITUAL (Monday 9 AM, 90 minutes)
[1] 9:00-9:20:  MEASURE (pull metrics from database)
[2] 9:20-9:45:  ANALYZE (identify #1 blocker)
[3] 9:45-10:10: HYPOTHESIS (what small change will fix it?)
[4] 10:10-10:30: PRIORITIZE (plan the experiment)

EXECUTION (Mon-Fri)
[+] Mon-Thu: Build the change, test locally
[+] Fri: Deploy to production, measure impact
[+] Next Monday: Measure results, decide keep/iterate/revert

MEASUREMENT BEFORE SHIPPING
Every change must:
[+] Have a measurable metric
[+] Be testable (A/B, before/after)
[+] Target 10%+ improvement minimum
[+] Be tracked in metrics database
